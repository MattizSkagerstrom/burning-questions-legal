Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

Burning Questions ("we", "our", or "the app") is a party game designed to be enjoyed without an account. We take a privacy-first approach: we collect as little as possible, we do not sell your data, and most game data lives on your device. This Privacy Policy explains what is collected, how it is used, and the choices you have.

1. Data we collect

  • On-device data. Player names, custom decks, language, settings, and trial state are stored locally on your device. They never leave the device unless you choose to share them.
  • Purchase data. When you subscribe to Premium, the purchase is processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We receive a receipt confirming entitlement, but not your payment method. Refer to Apple's or Google's privacy policies for how they handle payment information.
  • Advertising data. Free users see ads from third-party networks (e.g. Google AdMob). These networks may collect a device advertising identifier and basic technical data (IP address, device type, OS version) to serve and measure ads. On iOS, ad personalization requires your explicit consent via App Tracking Transparency.
  • Diagnostic data. We may receive anonymous crash and performance reports through the app store you installed from. These are not linked to your identity.

2. What we do not collect

We do not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, contacts, photos, location, microphone, or camera. We do not sell personal data.

3. How data is used

  • To run the game and remember your preferences.
  • To validate Premium entitlements with the app store.
  • To show ads to free users and (where you've consented) measure their performance.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve stability.

4. Third-party services

We rely on the following third parties. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play (purchases, receipts, diagnostics)
  • Google AdMob (advertising, for free users)

5. Children

The app contains adult-oriented content, including drinking themes and a "Spicy 18+" deck. It is intended for users aged 18 and over and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Your choices

  • Reset on-device data. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data, including custom decks and settings.
  • Ad personalization. On iOS, manage tracking via Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking. On Android, manage your advertising ID via Settings → Google → Ads.
  • Subscription. Manage or cancel Premium in your App Store or Play Store account settings.

7. Data retention

On-device data is kept until you remove it or uninstall the app. Purchase records are retained by the app store per their policies. Anonymous diagnostic and ad-measurement data are retained by their respective providers per their policies.

8. International users

The app is offered globally. Where required, the legal basis for processing is your consent (for personalized ads) and the performance of our agreement with you (to deliver the game and Premium features).

9. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out to the developer through the support contact listed on the app's store page.
